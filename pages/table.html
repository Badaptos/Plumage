Tables

Using regular HTML, Plumage gives you responsive tables that look great on any size screen.

Basic

HTML

<div class="col-md-12">
    <table>
        <caption>Developer Email List</caption>
        <thead>
            <tr>
                <th>First Name</th>
                <th>Last Name</th>
                <th>Email</th>
            </tr>
        </thead >
        <tbody>
            <tr>
                <td data-label="First Name">>Alex</td>
                <td data-label="Last Name">>Smith</td>
                <td data-label="Email">>asmith@plumage</td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
                <td data-label="First Name">>Natalia</td>
                <td data-label="Last Name">>Jones</td>
                <td data-label="Email">>njones@plumage</td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
                <td data-label="First Name">>Paulo</td>
                <td data-label="Last Name">>Razil</td>
                <td data-label="Email">>prazil@plumage</td>
            </tr>
        </tbody>
    </table>
</div>
Result
Developer Email List
First Name Last Name Email
Alex Smith alsmith@plumage
Natalia Jones najones@plumage
Paulo Brazil pabrazil@plumage

Border

Add the class of .border to <table> to give more definition.

HTML
<table class="border">
        <thead>. . .
