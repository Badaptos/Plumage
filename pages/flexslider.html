Simple Slider

Plumage makes it easy to show off images and more with this jquery flexslider.
Just add this script and two classes to your html.

Want captions? Add one more class, for a total of three, and you are good to go.

Script
<script type="text/javascript">
    $(document).ready(function(){
        $('.flexslider').flexslider({
            animation: "slide"
        });
    });
</script>
HTML
<section class="row">
    <div class="col-sm-12"></div>
    <div class="col-md-2"> </div>
        <article class="col-md-8">
        <div class="flexslider">
            <ul class="slides">
                <li><img src="images/1.jpg" alt=""></li>
                <li><img src="images/2.jpg" alt="">
                    <p class="flex-caption">Add a caption</p> </li>>
                <li><p class="flex-caption txt-center">Center the text </p>
                    <img src="images/3.jpg" alt=""></li>
                <li><img src="images/4.jpg" alt="">
                    <p class="flex-caption txt-right">Or right-hand justify/p>
                </li>
                <li><img src="images/5.jpg" alt=""></li>
            </ul>
        </div>
        </article>
    <div class="col-md-2"> </div> 
</section>
